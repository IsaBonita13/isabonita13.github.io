About Me

Hello! I'm Isabel. I'm deeply passionate about my Catholic faith and see beauty as a powerful means of evangelization. My goal is to use my skills in digital marketing and content creation to serve mission focused organizations.

I thrive in collaborative, fast paced environments where I can bring ideas to life. I believe that strategic, beautiful, and thoughtful content can connect with audiences on a deeper level. I'm organized, detail oriented, and excited by the opportunity to help a mission grow through creative and impactful marketing.
